# Due Diligence Checklist — PikaRobot
> Ref: YC Series A Diligence + Sequoia + a16z

## Financial Due Diligence
- [ ] Historical P&L (nếu có)
- [ ] Cash flow projections 3-5 năm
- [ ] Cap table hiện tại
- [ ] Burn rate & runway
- [ ] Tax filings
- [ ] Outstanding debt/liabilities
- [ ] Unit economics validation

## Legal Due Diligence
- [ ] Certificate of Incorporation
- [ ] Articles of Association
- [ ] IP Assignment Agreements (founders → company)
- [ ] Employee/contractor agreements
- [ ] Privacy Policy & Terms of Service
- [ ] Data protection compliance (đặc biệt: dữ liệu trẻ em)
- [ ] Trademark/Patent filings
- [ ] Outstanding lawsuits/disputes

## Product & Technology
- [ ] Product demo / walkthrough
- [ ] Tech stack review
- [ ] Code quality assessment
- [ ] Scalability architecture
- [ ] Data security measures
- [ ] AI model accuracy/performance benchmarks

## Market & Customers
- [ ] Market size validation (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- [ ] Customer interviews (5-10 parents/schools)
- [ ] Competitive analysis completed
- [ ] Pricing validation
- [ ] Distribution channel assessment

## Team
- [ ] Founder reference checks (3+ per founder)
- [ ] Background checks
- [ ] Vesting schedules confirmed
- [ ] Key person risk assessment
- [ ] Advisory board quality

## Business Model
- [ ] Revenue model clarity
- [ ] Customer acquisition strategy
- [ ] Retention strategy
- [ ] Expansion revenue potential
- [ ] Path to profitability timeline
